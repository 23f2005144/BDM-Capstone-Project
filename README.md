## 📌 About the BDM Capstone Project
A capstone project is a comprehensive, independent research project conducted to showcase the understanding of theoretical concepts. This project was based on Business Data Management (BDM), spanning six months and focused on analyzing and optimizing revenue for a multinational freight forwarding company operating across India. The work involved data collection, cleaning, analysis, and the development of actionable strategies to address business challenges 📈.

## 🎯 Problem Statement
For several years, the company has struggled to increase its revenue due to underutilized market segments, weak marketing practices, limited digital presence, and inefficient data management. This project seeks to overcome these challenges by analyzing sales performance and proposing targeted solutions 📊.

## 🔍 Approach Across Three Stages
### 1. Proposal Stage
- Identified key factors contributing to revenue stagnation, including untapped markets, ineffective marketing, and lack of digital tools 🧭
- Outlined the methodology for data collection, analysis, and reporting 📋

### 2. Mid-Term Stage
- Collected and cleaned sales data from Direct Shipments, focusing on revenue, profit, and shipment metrics 🧹
- Conducted descriptive analysis to uncover early trends and preliminary patterns, including segment and ratio analysis 📉
- Evaluated current marketing and CRM practices of the company to identify gaps 📚

### 3. Final-Term Stage
- Performed in-depth quantitative and qualitative analysis, including trend, cross-segment, and KPI (Key Performance Indicator) analysis to diagnose key issues and prescribe solutions 📊
- Developed actionable recommendations for improving revenue, marketing, and digital presence 🌐
- Presented findings and proposed solutions to support strategic decision-making 🎯

## 💡 Recommendations
- Focus on high-value segments and underperforming branches to maximize revenue potential 🎯
- Invest in robust data management and digital CRM systems to streamline operations 🖥️
- Enhance digital marketing and website optimization to improve market reach 🌐
- Utilize targeted marketing strategies to drive growth and customer engagement 📣
